Is Mold Testing Necessary

Mold testing on property that has a mold issue can offer some beneficial information, even if there is visible mold. It can give some insight as to whether or not the visible mold is causing an indoor air quality issue and just how high the levels might be. Also if you plan on using insurance to cover the work, it is definitely recommended.

At the same time, most people are usually asking this question because mold testing costs money and they are weighing whether or not they want to pay for it. To that we typically say if you have visible mold, you can usually go directly into the mold removal and remediation process. Regardless of what type of mold or the levels, we will be using the same abatement process. However, we always recommend clearance testing to verify the work has been completed and safe to reoccupy an area.

How Much does Albuquerque Mold Removal Cost?

The average cost of a mold remediation in Albuquerque is $1000-5000.

It is difficult to say where your project might fit on that scale without seeing the property but you can get a good idea based on what has been damaged.

  1. Walls
  2. Flooring
  3. Ceiling
  4. Cabinets
  5. Multiple Rooms
  6. Tile Walls
  7. Growth on Contents

The more of the items that you check off on that list, the higher the total mold removal cost is going to be.

While we almost always recommend doing a mold remediation with equipment left for multiple days so your job is guaranteed to pass a clearance test.
